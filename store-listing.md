## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

ReadyToFly is your personal pre-flight companion, built for frequent flyers who want to travel smarter and stress-free.

Add your flight once and ReadyToFly organises everything around your trip — from when to leave home, to what to pack, to what's allowed in your baggage.

FEATURES:

✈️ Flight Tracker
Track any flight in real time with a live countdown to your departure.

⏰ Leave-By Calculator
Enter your home address and get the exact time you need to leave to reach the airport comfortably.

📋 Document Checklist
Never forget a travel document again. Build your personalised checklist and tick items off before you head out.

🧳 Baggage Rules
Check your airline's exact cabin and check-in baggage allowances before you pack.

🗺️ Airport Guide
Explore terminals, gates, and facilities at your departure airport.

🌍 Visa Requirements
Instantly check visa requirements for your destination country.

🏆 Frequent Flyer Tracker
Track your miles and loyalty points across multiple airlines in one place.

🧘 Calm Mode
Guided breathing exercises designed specifically for nervous and anxious flyers.

🔍 Smart Search
Search any feature instantly — even with typos. Type bagage or airprot and ReadyToFly knows what you mean.

Built for Indian frequent flyers and international travellers. ReadyToFly works for every airline, every airport, every destination.

Download ReadyToFly and never have a stressful departure again.
