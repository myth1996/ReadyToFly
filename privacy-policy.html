Privacy Policy

App: ReadyToFly  |  Developer: Myth96  |  Last updated: March 2026

ReadyToFly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it. By using ReadyToFly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone number — collected during OTP-based sign-in (Indian +91 numbers) for authentication purposes only.
  • Flight details — flight numbers, dates, and airports you enter are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data — anonymised analytics events (screens viewed, features used) collected via Firebase Analytics to help us improve the app.
  • Crash reports — anonymous crash and performance data collected via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Advertising identifiers — your device's advertising ID, used by Google AdMob to show relevant ads (free users only). You can reset or opt out of ad personalisation in your device settings.

2. Third-Party Services

ReadyToFly uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — phone authentication, analytics, and crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob (Google LLC) — advertising SDK used to display ads to free users. Google Privacy Policy. AdMob may collect device information, location data, and advertising identifiers to serve personalised ads.
  • Razorpay — payment processing for ReadyToFly Premium subscriptions. Razorpay handles all payment data; we do not store card or banking details. Razorpay Privacy Policy.
  • AviationStack API — real-time flight data lookups. Your flight search queries (flight number, date) are sent to AviationStack's servers to retrieve flight status information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and keep your session secure.
  • To provide and improve app features.
  • To display advertisements to free-tier users (via AdMob).
  • To process Premium subscription payments (via Razorpay).
  • To diagnose bugs and crashes through anonymised reports.
  • We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Flight data and checklists are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Firebase Authentication retains your phone number for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account by contacting us at the email below.
  • Analytics data is retained by Google Firebase for up to 14 months in aggregated, anonymised form.

5. Advertising and Ad Personalisation

Free users see ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your advertising ID and general location to show personalised ads. You can opt out of ad personalisation:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

We comply with Google's Families Policy and do not direct targeted advertising at children under 13.

6. Children's Privacy

ReadyToFly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including Firebase's encrypted authentication and HTTPS-only API communications. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello.readytofly@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of ReadyToFly after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
